Boosts Memory:
Sleep strengthens and consolidates memories, improving learning and recall.
Improves Focus:
Adequate sleep enhances concentration and decision-making skills.
Reduces Stress:
Quality sleep helps regulate stress hormones, promoting emotional stability.
Supports Immunity:
Sleep enhances your immune system, helping to fight off illness.
Aids Recovery:
During sleep, your body repairs tissues and builds muscle.
Regulates Weight:
Proper sleep balances hunger hormones, reducing the risk of weight gain.
